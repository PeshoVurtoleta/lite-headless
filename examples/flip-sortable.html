how it works

Two functions: flipBefore(els) reads every item's getBoundingClientRect() and stashes it in a Map. flipAfter(rects) reads the NEW rects after the DOM mutation, computes the delta, applies transform: translate(dx, dy) without a transition (so the items appear unchanged), then on the next two animation frames clears the transform with a transition — the browser animates the difference.

Wrapped around drag (via dragstart + reorder events) and around imperative calls (via the withFlip(fn) helper). Zero deps. ~30 LOC.
